Booking Guidelines

Please review the following before booking an appointment:

  • ✅ Be clear about your project idea. The more details you provide, the better we can help.
  • ✅ Select the right service. Choose whether you need help with a concept, mechanics, visuals, or full development.
  • ✅ Respect scheduled times. Missed appointments may need to be rescheduled.
  • ✅ Payments & Fees: Initial consultations are free. Project-based pricing is discussed during the meeting.
