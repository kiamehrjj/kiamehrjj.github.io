About CPT Game Designers

CPT Game Designers was founded by passionate creators who believe in the power of games to entertain, teach, and inspire. With years of experience in both independent and collaborative game design, our mission is to help others turn their concepts into reality.

We work with:

  • Solo developers and hobbyists
  • Educational institutions
  • Startups and established companies

From board games to digital games, we bring creative, user-focused designs to every project.
